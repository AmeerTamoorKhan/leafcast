Why Existing Methods Fail

Traditional generative models are ill-suited for field phenotyping applications.

Hardware Incompatibility

Field deployment relies on smartphones, drones, and robots with limited battery and compute. Heavy GPU-based models like Pix2Pix drain resources instantly.

Inefficient Resource Allocation

Plants only occupy 10-20% of the image. Standard models waste massive capacity reconstructing static backgrounds (soil, pots) with full fidelity.
