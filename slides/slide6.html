Inverted Residual Backbone

Uses MobileNetV2-style inverted residual blocks instead of standard convolutions. This significantly reduces parameter count while maintaining representational capacity.

Encoder-Decoder Design

Follows a U-Net inspired structure with skip connections. Features are progressively downsampled to 12x12 bottleneck and upsampled back to 192x192.

Edge-Ready Inference

The heavy Discriminator is discarded after training. Only the lightweight Generator (3.9M params) is deployed, enabling <100ms inference on edge devices.
