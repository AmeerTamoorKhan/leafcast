Green-Aware Input Encoding

Standard convolutions treat all color channels equally. LeafCast applies learnable channel weights initialized to emphasize green (chlorophyll) signals immediately.

Includes a parallel texture path with depthwise separable convolutions to capture structural details early.

Biological Bias

Plant Attention Gates

Combines spatial and channel attention to focus computation solely on the plant, suppressing the 80%+ of pixels that are static background.

y = (1-α)·x + α·xattended

Learnable blend (α) ensures stability.

Resource Focus

Texture Refinement

Lightweight models often blur high-frequency details. This block uses parallel branches with different dilation rates (1, 2, 3) to capture multi-scale texture cues.

Strategically placed at 48×48 and 96×96 decoder stages to recover venation and leaf boundaries.
